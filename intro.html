Mission

Waste-Os mission is to be committed to making everyone aware of Improper Waste Management and instill discipline, knowledge, and practices to people who are using the app to have a cleaner environment for everyone.

Vision

Waste-Os vision is to expand to different communities and to create more environmental applications so that Improper Waste Management will be reduced in the Philippines.
